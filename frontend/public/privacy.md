## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

**Account Information:**
- Email address (required for account creation)
- Display name or username (if provided)
- Public avatar URL (if it exists)
- Password (optional, stored in encrypted form)
- Profile information you choose to provide

**Communications:**
- Messages you send to us via email or contact forms
- Support requests and correspondence

### Information from Third-Party Services

**OAuth2 Providers:**
- If you sign in using a 3rd party Provider such as (but not limited to) Google, LinkedIn, X, GitHub, TikTok, Facebook, Discord, etc., we receive your email address and basic profile information from that provider.
- We do not access your 3rd party Provider account beyond the initial authentication

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

**To Provide and Maintain Our Service:**
- Create and manage your account

**To Improve Our Service:**
- Understand how users interact with our platform
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Develop new features and functionality
- Conduct analytics and research (may be implemented in the future)

**To Communicate With You:**
- Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password resets)
- Notify you about subscription renewals or expirations
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Send important service announcements

**To Ensure Security and Prevent Fraud:**
- Protect against unauthorized access
- Enforce our Terms of Service

**For Compliance:**
- Comply with legal obligations
- Respond to legal requests and prevent harm

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our Service. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 90 days, unless we are required to retain it for legal or compliance purposes.
